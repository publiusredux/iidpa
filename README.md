# Immigration Integrity and Due Process Act (IIDPA)
This repository contains the legislative packet for the **Immigration Integrity and Due Process Act (IIDPA)** — a citizen-drafted framework for restoring control, fairness, and constitutional due process in the U.S. immigration system.

## Contents
- **[Executive_Summary.md](Executive_Summary.md)** – High-level overview of the problem, goals, and core reforms.
- **[One_Pager.md](One_Pager.md)** – Single-page handout with key points and talking tracks.
- **[IIDPA_Full_Bill_Text.md](IIDPA_Full_Bill_Text.md)** – Full draft bill text, organized by titles and sections.
- **[Section_By_Section_Summary.md](Section_By_Section_Summary.md)** – Detailed breakdown of each section's purpose and impact.
- **[Simulated_CBO_Score.md](Simulated_CBO_Score.md)** – Rough, good-faith fiscal and impact estimates.
- **[PRESS_RELEASE.md](PRESS_RELEASE.md)** – Sample press release for outreach.
- **[CONTRIBUTORS.md](CONTRIBUTORS.md)** – Guidelines for submitting feedback or edits.
- **[LICENSE](LICENSE)** – Reuse and contribution terms.

## How to Use This Repository
- **Legislative staff & policymakers:** Start with the **[Executive Summary](Executive_Summary.md)** or **[One Pager](One_Pager.md)**, then review the **[Full Bill Text](IIDPA_Full_Bill_Text.md)** and **[Simulated CBO Score](Simulated_CBO_Score.md)** for detail.
- **Researchers, advocates, and citizens:** Use this as a starting point for discussion, critique, and improvement. Suggestions and technical edits are welcome—see **[CONTRIBUTORS.md](CONTRIBUTORS.md)** for details.
