# Contributors Guide

Thank you for your interest in improving the Immigration Integrity and Due Process Act (IIDPA). This project encourages constructive, good-faith collaboration.

---

## **How to Submit Suggestions**

### **1. Open an Issue**
Use GitHub Issues for:
- Technical edits  
- Clarity improvements  
- Policy critiques  
- Citation or legal-reference suggestions  

### **2. Submit a Pull Request**
For direct edits:
- Fork the repository  
- Make your changes  
- Submit a pull request with a brief explanation  

### **3. Start a Discussion**
For larger proposals—new sections, alternative pathway designs, or complex legal questions—open a Discussion thread before drafting.

---

## **Review Process**
- Pull requests are typically reviewed within **7–10 days**  
- Substantial changes (structural, definitional, fiscal) may require discussion  
- All reviews prioritize accuracy, clarity, and constitutional coherence  

---

## **Style Notes**
- Use legally clear, concise, and neutral language  
- Cite INA or U.S. Code sections where appropriate  
- Prefer gender-neutral language (“they” rather than “he/she”)  
- Avoid rhetorical flourish—aim for statutory precision  
- Use consistent formatting: headings, section numbers, and lists  

---

## **Code of Conduct**
- Assume good faith  
- Critique ideas, not individuals  
- Maintain civility  
- No partisan labeling or personal attacks  

Thank you for helping refine this framework.
