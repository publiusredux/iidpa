# IIDPA — Analytical Documentation

This directory contains supplementary legal and policy analysis supporting the Immigration Integrity and Due Process Act (IIDPA).

These documents are intended for legal-technical review and are published alongside the bill to demonstrate awareness of known vulnerabilities and implementation challenges.

## Purpose of Failure Mode Reviews

No legislative draft of this complexity survives first contact with litigation, implementation, or political change without known vulnerabilities. These reviews are published not to undermine the bill but to:

- Identify where legal challenges are most likely and from whom
- Map the sequence in which implementation would fail under resource or coordination constraints
- Anticipate how regulatory drift could erode statutory intent between administrations

The goal is a bill that is resilient by design, not just on paper.

---

## Relationship to the Bill

These documents are analytical supplements. They do not modify the bill text. Findings that warrant statutory fixes are incorporated into the bill directly via the main `IIDPA_Full_Bill_Text.md` and tracked in `CHANGELOG.md`.
